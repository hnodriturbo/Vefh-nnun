Avatar is a 2009 science fiction movie directed by James Cameron. The plot follows a former Marine named Jake Sully who is sent to the planet Pandora to gain the trust of the native Na'vi people, as a corporation aims to mine a valuable mineral from their home. To do so, Sully uses an avatar, a genetically engineered Na'vi body controlled by a human mind. As Sully becomes more involved with the Na'vi and falls in love with a Na'vi woman named Neytiri, he is faced with a difficult decision.
