About IT Conference

Hosted by Hreiðar Pétursson, the IT Conference is an annual event presented by the students of Tækniskólinn. This conference serves as a beacon for innovation, showcasing cutting-edge research and development in information technology. This year, we delve into the theme "Navigating the Digital Frontier," exploring the latest trends in cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and software development. Join us for a transformative experience filled with engaging talks, interactive sessions, and collaborative workshops that will shape the future of technology.
